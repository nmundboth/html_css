Want to strengthen your networking? Meet the right people!



At WebCareer, you have the opportunity to meet employers and people who can become important referrals to you. We organise meetings on the last day of our training sessions. If you are not registered in a training session but still want referrals, you can attend this event by registering through the agency first.


  • Attending through training session: Free

  • Attending through agency and not a WebCareer client: $30 entrance fee
